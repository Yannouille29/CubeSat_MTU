About Cubesat

A CubeSat is a type of miniature cube-shaped satellite. They are used for low-cost space missions, scientific research, technology development, or educational purposes. CubeSats have standardized sizes, typically measured in "U" units (1U, 2U, 3U, etc.), with each unit being approximately 10x10x10 cm.

Nimbus Project

Under the supervision and guidance of JUAN FRANCISCO MARTINEZ, a group of trainees in the fields of electronics and computer science actively joined a research project at the MTU research center, known as NIMBUS. This internship experience at the NIMBUS center offers valuable opportunities for these trainees to gain significant hands-on experience and make meaningful contributions to technological advancements within the research team.
